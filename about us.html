Meet Dorry

Welcome to Dory’s Doughy Donuts, I'm Doreen the heart and soul behind this small business, dedicated to sharing my Donuts with everyone.

From a young age, I watched my mom and grandmother lovingly prepare traditional Polish dishes, instilling in me a profound respect for quality and authenticity. Today, I carry on their treasured family recipes, handcrafting these delights from scratch using all-natural ingredients, without shortcuts or artificial additives.

At Dory’s, our mission is simple: to bring joy with every bite. We take great pride in crafting donuts that are familiar yet exciting, offering you a taste experience that is both comforting and new. We can’t wait to share our donuts with you, and we hope to bring a smile to your face with every bite.
