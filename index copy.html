Dory's Doughy Donuts

At Dory’s, we are driven by a passion for creating donuts that are not only delicious but infused with the warmth and authenticity of African heritage. Our donuts are a celebration of African-inspired flavors, handcrafted with love using the finest ingredients.
As a home bakery, we believe in quality and craft. Each donut is made-to-order, ensuring freshness without shortcuts or additives. From our home-made fillings to the dough itself, every element is created from scratch in small batches. We pride ourselves on using only quality ingredients, no preservatives, softeners, or stabilizers, ever.
The inspiration behind our menu comes from a rich blend of global culinary experiences and the deep connection to the culture and traditions of my African roots. Having traveled the world as an international cabin crew , I’ve combined my appreciation for global flavors with the rich, vibrant tastes of home.

At Dory’s, our mission is simple: to bring joy with every bite. We take great pride in crafting donuts that are familiar yet exciting, offering you a taste experience that is both comforting and new. We can’t wait to share our donuts with you, and we hope to bring a smile to your face with every bite.

FAQS

frequently asked questions and answers.

How do I get my hands on your donuts?

We take orders throughout the week until Friday. Pick up or Delivery at a small fee depending on distance is on Saturday or Sunday afternoon. We rotate our donut flavors every 2-3 weeks and offer four flavors at a time. Check our full menu for an updated list.

Do you accept cash?

We gladly accept cash, card payments, as well as Zelle, CashApp, and Venmo. If paying with cash, kindly bring the exact amount, as we may not always have change available."

Do you take large/special orders (for weddings, birthdays, etc.)?

We currently accept special orders for our jumbo filled or glazed and for large quantities of donuts (24 donut minimum).

I don't live in Valdosta. Do you ship?

No. Quality is extremely important to us, and since our products are made without preservatives, softeners, stabilizers, and the like, they will not hold up well through shipping.

Do your products contain dairy/eggs?

"Yes, our donuts, cookies, and brownies contain eggs and butter, and most of our donut fillings include cream. For other allergens, please note we fry our donuts in non-GMO rice bran oil, and we are not a nut-free bakery.

Do you offer vegan donuts?

We do have a vegan option—Beignets (African-style donuts) made with coconut milk and cardamom and are completely vegan. Unfortunately, at this time we are unable to offer gluten-free donuts.
