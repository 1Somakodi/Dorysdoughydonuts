Frequently asked questions and answers.

How do I get my hands on your donuts?

We take orders throughout the week until Friday. You can Pick up the order or have it delivered to you at a small fee depending on distance. We deliver to locations within 5 miles radius of Ray City. We rotate our donut flavors every 2-3 weeks. Check our full menu for an updated list.

Do you accept cash?

We gladly accept cash, card payments, as well as Zelle or Venmo or CashApp. If paying with cash, kindly bring the exact amount, as we may not always have change available.

Do you take large/special orders (for weddings, birthdays.)?

We currently accept special orders for our jumbo filled or glazed donuts, with a minimum order of 24 donuts.

I don't live in Valdosta. Do you ship?

No. Quality is extremely important to us, and since our products are made without preservatives, softeners, stabilizers, and the like, they will not hold up well through shipping. We would never sacrifice quality for convenience. We hope you’ll be able to make an order when you visit the local area.

Do your products contain dairy/eggs?

Yes, our donuts, cookies, and brownies contain eggs and butter, and most of our donut fillings include cream. For other allergens, please note we fry our donuts in non-GMO rice bran oil, and we are not a nut-free bakery.

Do you offer vegan donuts?

We do have a vegan option—Beignets (African-style donuts) made with coconut milk and cardamom and are completely vegan.

Do you offer Gluten Free donuts?

Unfortunately, at this time we are unable to offer gluten-free donuts.
