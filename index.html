A little about Me.

Welcome to Dory’s Doughy Donuts, I'm Doreen the heart and soul behind this small business, dedicated to sharing my Donuts with everyone. From a young age, I watched my mom and grandmother lovingly prepare traditional Polish dishes, instilling in me a profound respect for quality and authenticity. Today, I carry on their treasured family recipes, handcrafting these delights from scratch using all-natural ingredients, without shortcuts or artificial additives.

At Dory’s, our mission is simple: to bring joy with every bite. We take great pride in crafting donuts that are familiar yet exciting, offering you a taste experience that is both comforting and new. We can’t wait to share our donuts with you, and we hope to bring a smile to your face with every bite. We are driven by a passion for creating donuts that are not only delicious but infused with the warmth and authenticity of African heritage. Our donuts are a celebration of African-inspired flavors, handcrafted with love using the finest ingredients.

As a home bakery, we believe in quality and craft. Each donut is made-to-order, ensuring freshness without shortcuts or additives. From our home-made fillings to the dough itself, every element is created from scratch in small batches. We pride ourselves on using only quality ingredients, no preservatives, softeners, or stabilizers, ever.

The inspiration behind our menu comes from a rich blend of global culinary experiences and the deep connection to the culture and traditions of my African roots. Having traveled the world as an international cabin crew , I’ve combined my appreciation for global flavors with the rich, vibrant tastes of home. At Dory’s, our mission is simple: to bring joy with every bite. We take great pride in crafting donuts that are familiar yet exciting, offering you a taste experience that is both comforting and new. We can’t wait to share our donuts with you, and we hope to bring a smile to your face with every bite.
