Dory's Doughy Donuts

A little about Us.

At Dory’s, we create donuts that blend the rich flavors of African heritage with a dash of global inspiration. Made fresh to order, every donut is crafted with love, using only the finest ingredients—no preservatives, no shortcuts. From the dough to the fillings, everything is made from scratch in small batches, ensuring pure, irresistible flavor.

Inspired by my travels as international cabin crew and my African roots, our menu offers a unique taste journey that’s both familiar and exciting. At Dory’s, our mission is simple: to bring joy with every bite. We can’t wait to share our donuts with you!
