Jumbo Donuts

Our donuts come in two irresistible options: filled with rich, creamy flavors or topped with a glossy glaze for that extra touch of sweetness. Each donut weighs about 75g, making them the ideal size for a delicious, satisfying bite.

African Donuts

Mandazi is a beloved East African treat—crispy on the outside, soft on the inside, and lightly sweet. Similar to a donut, but with a unique twist, made triangular or round, are infused with coconut milk and a hint of cardamom.

Available Donut Glazing: Chocolate, Cinnamon Sugar, Classic Vanilla Glaze
Available Donut Toppings: Nuts, Coconut flakes, chocolate shavings, Sprinkles, Biscoff, Oreos (0.50 cents extra)
Available Donut Fillings: Strawberry Jam, Chocolate frosting, Oreo frosting, Peanut Butter frosting, Biscoff frosting.
